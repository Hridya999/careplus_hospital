Intensive Cardiac Care Unit (ICCU)

About Our ICCU

Our Intensive Cardiac Care Unit (ICCU) is a state-of-the-art critical care facility designed to provide specialized treatment for patients with severe cardiac conditions. Equipped with advanced monitoring systems, ventilators, and a 24/7 team of experienced cardiologists and nurses, the ICCU ensures prompt and precise care.

The unit follows strict infection control protocols, providing a sterile and safe environment. Each patient is continuously monitored using advanced cardiac monitoring technology, and family members are updated regularly by our medical team.

Operating Hours

24 Hours / 7 Days

Room Services

24/7 Nursing, Medication, and Attendant Assistance

Available Equipment
